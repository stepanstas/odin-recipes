Steak Tartare

Description

Steak tartare is a dish made from raw ground meat/beef that is served in many different countries and in many different ways. It is very popular in my home country of the Czech Republic. Steak tartare is usually served with onions, capers, mushrooms, pepper, or other seasoning of choice. Fresh raw egg yolk is often served on top of the steak.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of top sirloin from organic grass-fed and grass-finished beef
  • 1 egg yolk from organic, pasture-raised/free-range eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 fresh minced or chopped garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh or dried chives or other herb/seasoning of choice
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh red onions

Steps

  1. Grind the meat or chop it finely with a knife.
  2. Mix in the salt, pepper, paprika, onions, and herb of choice.
  3. Shape the meat into a patty and put it in the center of a plate.
  4. Make a small indent in the patty and place the fresh egg yolk in it.
  5. Serve and enjoy the tremendous power and energy from all of the nutrients that are present in the raw meat and eggs.
